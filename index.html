Thanh Dinh Ta - Verimag

About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Joseph Fourier University, working in the PACSS group at the laboratory Verimag. I have completed my PhD at Lorraine University, in the CARTE group at the laboratory Loria.

My research focuses on the binary code analysis and on the detection of malwares. I am interested in formal models of programs and their applications in extracting high-level properties from obfuscated binary codes.
